Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. BrandPilot AI is committed to protecting your personal information while delivering intelligent AI-powered personal branding, content generation, calendar automation, and analytics.

1. Information We Collect

To provide personalized AI recommendations, BrandPilot AI collects only the necessary information required for the platform.

  • User profile information
  • Professional details and niche
  • Content publishing history
  • Calendar events (with permission)
  • Engagement statistics
  • Connected social media accounts
  • Usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to improve your experience and personalize our AI services.

  • Generate personalized content ideas
  • Create AI captions and hashtags
  • Recommend posting schedules
  • Analyze audience engagement
  • Improve AI accuracy
  • Optimize multi-platform publishing
  • Deliver smart reminders

3. AI & Calendar Access

BrandPilot AI accesses your Google Calendar only after receiving your explicit permission. Calendar data is used solely to identify opportunities for creating relevant content, scheduling posts, and providing reminders. We never modify calendar events without your approval.

4. Social Media Integration

When you connect LinkedIn, Instagram, X, or YouTube, BrandPilot AI accesses only the permissions required for publishing content, retrieving analytics, and improving recommendations. We never post automatically without your authorization.

5. Data Security

We protect your information using industry-standard security measures, including encrypted communication, secure authentication, and restricted access to sensitive data.

  • SSL Encryption
  • Secure Authentication
  • Cloud Data Protection
  • Access Control
  • Regular Security Monitoring

6. Your Privacy Rights

You remain in control of your personal information at all times.

  • View your stored information
  • Update your profile
  • Disconnect social accounts
  • Revoke calendar permissions
  • Delete your account
  • Request permanent data deletion

7. Ethical AI Commitment

BrandPilot AI follows responsible AI principles.

  • ❌ No fake engagement
  • ❌ No unethical scraping
  • ❌ No spam posting
  • ❌ No selling personal information
  • ✔ Transparent AI recommendations
  • ✔ User-controlled automation

8. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and analytics technologies to improve platform performance, understand user interactions, enhance personalization, and provide a smoother experience.

9. Updates to This Policy

Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect new features, security improvements, or legal requirements. Any significant changes will be communicated through the platform.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact the BrandPilot AI support team through the application's support or contact page.
